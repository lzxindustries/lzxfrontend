## Description

**Precision conditioning of external audio and control voltages to 0 to 1 Volt scale**

Voltage Interface I is a 5-channel precision voltage conditioner which can transform external signals, such as 10 Volt control voltages and line level audio, into the 1V signal ranges expected by LZX Visionary modules. Voltages below 0 Volts and above 1 Volt are safely clipped off, with LED indicators showing clipping behavior for both negative and positive clipping. Gain control offers a logarithmic control response, making it easier to attenuate 10 Volt peak-to-peak signals. The module may also be used as a low bandwidth video clipper and distortion device.
