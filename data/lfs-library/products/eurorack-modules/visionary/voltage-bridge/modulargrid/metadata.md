## Description

**Voltage Interface**

voltage bridge is a simple, straightforward 8-channel voltage scaling module designed for conveniently interfacing with synthesizer modules and devices outside of or integrated into an lzx visionary system. four sets of inputs/outputs attenuate a signal by 20% (5 volt scale to 1 volt scale), and another four sets of inputs/outputs increase the gain of a signal by 500% (1 volt to 5 volt scale.) this module can be used for any type of signal: audio, control voltage, logic, video, etc. aside from its utility purposes, it can be used to achieve various effects as well.
