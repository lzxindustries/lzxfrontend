## Description

**Synchronized waveform outputs for use in wipe and pattern generation**

ideo ramps is a dual synchronized waveform generator. one ramp generator is locked to the vertical interval of the video display (field-rate sync), and the other is locked to the horizontal interval (line-rate sync). four different output waveforms are available from each ramp generator, including sawtooth, inverted sawtooth, triangle, and inverted triangle. video ramps offers a minimal solution to augment your system's available waveform generation capabilities (such as video waveform generator modules), with outputs that are useful in almost any wipe or shape generation application. additionally, the outputs can be used to sweep the x &amp; y inputs of an oscilloscope or vector display for vector rescanning experimentation.

LZX sync cable required
