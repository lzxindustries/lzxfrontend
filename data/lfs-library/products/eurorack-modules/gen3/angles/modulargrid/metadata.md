## Description

**wall of ramps**

Angles is a 12HP ramp generator with 24 outputs at 15 degree rotary increments from 0 degrees (H-Ramp) to 345 degrees. It can also be used as a general purposed fixed ratio mixer by patching the inputs. It's a protractor for your patch!
