## Description

**Passive Multiples**

MLT is a passive utility module for distributing signals within your patch. The inputs cascade downward, providing up to thirty one outputs of a single input, or up to eight separate multiples with three outputs.

MLT is offered as a Fully Assembled module, Partial DIY Kit (PCB &amp; Panel Only) or a Full DIY Kit (all components included.)
