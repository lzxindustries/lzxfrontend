## Description

**4-channel wide bandwidth voltage scaler**

Cadet V Scaler is a 4-channel wide bandwidth voltage scaler which can be ordered or built as a DIY module for the purposes of several different standards conversions. Assembled modules are built for 5V to 1V scaling for EuroRack to LZX level translation. DIY kits provide parts for several other build options, including 1V to 5V amplification and unipolar/bipolar conversion. Cadet modules and kits are packed and built to order, please allow up to 2 weeks for shipment.
