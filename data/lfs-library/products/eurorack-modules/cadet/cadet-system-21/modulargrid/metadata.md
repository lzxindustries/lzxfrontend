## Description

**LZX-CSYS21-EUROMODULE**

Cadet System 21 is a 21-module (84HP) selection of basic video synthesis building blocks. Like the individual Cadet modules, it is available in a large variety of DIY and assembled options. Included in this set are (1) Cadet I Sync Generator, (1) Cadet II RGB Encoder, (1) Cadet III Video Input, (1) Cadet IV Dual Ramp Generator, (1) Cadet V Scaler, (4) Cadet VI Fader, (4) Cadet VII Processor, (4) Cadet VIII Hard Key Generator, and (4) Cadet IX Voltage Controlled Oscillator. This product will begin shipping on February 22nd, 2016.
