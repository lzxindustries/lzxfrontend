# Cadet IV Dual Ramp Generator

## Overview

Cadet IV Dual Ramp Generator is a dual waveform generator which creates 3 different fixed frequency ramp waveforms locked to the horizontal and vertical dimensions of a video display. The Cadet series of DIY Eurorack modules brings simple, analog DIY video synthesizer building blocks to your modular workspace.
