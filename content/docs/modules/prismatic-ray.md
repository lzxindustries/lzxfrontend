# Prismatic Ray

## Overview

No longer available. Prismatic Ray is the next generation of the LZX Industries analogue wide range Voltage Controlled Oscillator design. With a max frequency range into the megahertz and hard sync input with phase stability, it is uniquely qualified for video synthesis techniques. Width, 16HP Mounting Depth, 32mm Power +12V @ 120mA Power -12V @ 90mA
