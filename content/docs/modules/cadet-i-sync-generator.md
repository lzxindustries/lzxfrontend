# Cadet I Sync Generator

## Overview

DIY PCB & Frontpanel set comes with SMT Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator (VCXO) pre-soldered + ATMEGA88P Pre-Programmed. Cadet I Sync Generator (C1) generates the master timing signals required for your modular video synthesizer in both NTSC and PAL formats. For the Cadet series DIY analog video synthesizer system.
