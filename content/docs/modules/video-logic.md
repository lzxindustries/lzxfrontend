# Video Logic

## Overview

The first in our logic series. Extremely fast CMOS logic with high speed comparators on all inputs. Used for boolean logic operations on any type or frequency of input. Logic circuits are capable of pixel rate switching. A familiar arrangement for many modular synthesizers, the module includes two Boolean circuits with AND/OR/XOR outs and two logic inverters. With video logic and even just a couple simple sources, it's easy to turn a relatively simple pattern into many complex geometrically related outputs.
