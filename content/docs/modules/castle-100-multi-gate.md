# Castle 100 (Multi-Gate)

**Castle 100** groups **multiple 4000-series style** gates in one **4 HP** (series-typical) **panel** so you can form **NAND**, **NOR**, **XOR**, and **inversion** in place without a breadboard, keeping **5V** digital **noise** and **lead** length under control in a **video** rack.

## Role in a patch

Combine **shift-register** bits, **counter** lines, and **comparator** outputs into **Boolean** video shapes and **gating** before **DACs** or **Cadet** keying. Keep **rise times** and **cable** dress sane for clean edges at video-related rates.

## Power and build

Use **Castle** **power** and **bypassing** as specified for your PCB revision. Do not substitute **non-CMOS** part families where the design calls for **5V**-rated inputs.
