# Staircase

## Overview

Limited edition black panel . Ascending the five steps of Staircase can transform a simple gradient or video source into a dense cascade of interrelated patterns. Staircase applies the principles of analog waveshaping to create a multi-level continuous solarizer. Wonderful module for starter systems that grows with you as you build your video synthesis system. Width, 10HP Mounting Depth, 32mm Power +12V @ 70mA Power -12V @ 70mA
