# Cadet II RGB Encoder

## Overview

DIY PCB & Frontpanel set comes with SMT AD724 RGB Encoder IC pre-soldered. Cadet II RGB Encoder is an RGB-to-NTSC/PAL composite video encoder for video synthesis systems. For the Cadet series DIY Eurorack analog modular video synthesizer system.
