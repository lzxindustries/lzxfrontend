# Castle 111 (D Flip-Flops)

**Castle 111** provides **D-type** latches: **one-bit** **memory** that updates on a **clock** edge, connecting **counters** and **shift** paths to **synchronous** **state** in your **digital** **video** patch.

## Role in a patch

Use **111** to **register** a **condition**, **synchronize** a **gating** line to **pixel**-relevant **timing**, and **build** small **state machines** with **100 / 101** and **110**.

## Power and build

**Castle** **5V** logic; avoid **exceeding** **input** ratings on **clocks** and **D** when interfacing from **analog** sections—**buffer** or **condition** as in the community **reference** builds.
