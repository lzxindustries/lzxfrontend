# Double Vision 168

**Double Vision 168** is the complete 168 HP LZX system: a Vessel 168–class case, DC and sync distribution, and the same eight core video modules as the current Double Vision bundle (see the [Double Vision system manual](/instruments/double-vision) for the module list).

For the TBC2 Expander in newer bundles, see the [Double Vision Expander](/instruments/double-vision-expander) page and the [TBC2](/modules/tbc2) and [TBC2 Expander](/modules/tbc2-expander) module manuals.

Retail configuration and box contents are listed on the product Overview in the store; keep your order invoice and LZX support correspondence as the source of truth for your revision.
