faqItems:
  - question: 'What does the TBC2 Expander add to a TBC2?'
    answer: |
      It is a **front-panel extension** for [TBC2](/modules/tbc2) with **VGA** (and, on some revisions, other) input connectors, so you can bring in YPbPr-over-VGA and related signals. It does not replace the main TBC2—signal processing still happens on the TBC2 module. See the [TBC2 Expander manual](/modules/tbc2-expander/manual) and the main [TBC2 manual](/modules/tbc2/manual) for use.
  - question: 'Where do I get firmware, MIDI maps, and troubleshooting for inputs?'
    answer: |
      All of that is documented for the main **TBC2**—open the [TBC2 support](/modules/tbc2/support) page and the [TBC2 manual](/modules/tbc2/manual). The expander page documents installation and the expander’s role only.
