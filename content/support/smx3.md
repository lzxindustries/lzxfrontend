faqItems:
  - question: 'What is SMX3?'
    answer: |
      **SMX3** is a **nine-input, three-output summing matrix**: each input
      feeds three separate mix busses, with per-input gain from **-2× to +2×**
      and cross-routing to three independent outputs. See the
      [SMX3 manual](/modules/smx3/manual) for the full block diagram, norms,
      and patch ideas.
