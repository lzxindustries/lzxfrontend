faqItems:
  - question: 'What is PROC for?'
    answer: |
      PROC is a **triple voltage processor** and **summing amplifier** with
      continuous control over attenuation, inversion, and bias. You use it to
      scale, offset, and combine patchable signals (including 1V video and
      related control) across three identical channels, plus a central mix
      output. The full reference is in the [PROC manual](/modules/proc/manual).
