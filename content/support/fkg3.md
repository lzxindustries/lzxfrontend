faqItems:
  - question: 'What does FKG3 do?'
    answer: |
      FKG3 is a **fader and key generating compositor** for layering two RGB
      sources, with **internal** or **external** keying and a fader to blend
      between them. The panel reference, routing, and creative applications are
      covered in the [FKG3 manual](/modules/fkg3/manual).
