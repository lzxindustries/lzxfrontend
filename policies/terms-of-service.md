# Warranty Policy

LZX Industries LLC ("LZX") stands behind the quality of our products. This section describes your warranty coverage, how to file a claim, and what is and is not covered.

## Standard Warranty — Assembled Modules

All assembled modules sold by LZX Industries are covered by a **1-year limited warranty** from the date of delivery. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

During the warranty period, LZX will, at our discretion, **repair or replace** the defective product at no charge. Replacement products may be new or refurbished with equivalent functionality.

## Dead on Arrival (DOA) — Fast-Track Replacement

If your assembled module fails to power on, respond, or function correctly within **45 days of delivery**, it qualifies for our DOA fast-track program:

- Contact us with a brief description of the issue and photos or a short video demonstrating the problem.
- We will ship a replacement unit to you as quickly as possible.
- **LZX covers return shipping** for DOA claims. We will provide a prepaid return label or reimburse your return shipping costs.
- Return of the defective unit is required within 14 days of receiving your replacement.

The DOA fast-track is designed to get you up and running immediately, without the wait times of a standard repair.

## Warranty Coverage — DIY Kits

DIY kits (PCB and frontpanel sets) are covered as follows:

- **PCB and frontpanel manufacturing defects** — such as faulty traces, delamination, drill errors, or silkscreen defects — are covered for **1 year** from delivery.
- **Pre-populated SMT components** (where applicable) are covered for **1 year** from delivery against manufacturing defects.
- **Assembly errors** are not covered. The warranty does not apply to damage caused by incorrect soldering, wrong component placement, solder bridges, or other assembly mistakes.

If you believe your DIY kit has a manufacturing defect, contact us with photos of the issue. We will evaluate your claim and, if the defect is confirmed, provide a replacement PCB, panel, or component as appropriate.

## What Is Not Covered

This warranty does not cover:

- Damage caused by **incorrect power supply** voltage, polarity, or connection (including damage from faulty eurorack bus boards or power supplies).
- Damage caused by **physical impact**, drops, or crushing.
- Damage caused by **liquid exposure** or environmental contamination.
- Damage caused by **user modifications**, unauthorized repairs, or alterations to the product.
- **Cosmetic wear** — including scratches, scuffs, patina, or fading from normal use — that does not affect functionality.
- Damage resulting from use with **incompatible equipment** or outside the product's specified operating conditions.
- Products with **removed, altered, or illegible serial numbers**.
- Products purchased from **unauthorized dealers** or secondhand sellers who cannot provide proof of original purchase.
- **DIY assembly errors** — see DIY Kits section above.

## Warranty Claim Process

To file a warranty claim:

1. **Email us** at **support@lzxindustries.net** with:

   - Your order number or proof of purchase.
   - The product name and serial number (if applicable).
   - A description of the issue.
   - Photos or a short video demonstrating the problem.

2. **We will respond** within 1–2 business days with a determination:

   - If the issue is covered, we will provide return shipping instructions (or a prepaid label for DOA claims).

   - If the issue is not covered under warranty, we will explain why and discuss repair options, which may be available for a fee.

3. **Ship the product to us** using a trackable shipping method. For standard warranty claims (non-DOA), the customer is responsible for shipping costs to LZX. LZX covers return shipping of the repaired or replaced product to you.

4. **Repair turnaround:** We aim to complete warranty repairs within **10 business days** of receiving your product. If a longer timeline is needed, we will notify you.
