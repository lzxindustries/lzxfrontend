## Order & Shipping Questions

For order status, tracking, address changes, or shipping inquiries, email us at **support@lzxindustries.net** with your order number. See our [Shipping Policy](/policies/shipping-policy) for processing times and delivery estimates.

## Returns & Warranty Claims

To request a return or report a warranty issue, email **support@lzxindustries.net** with your order number and a description of the issue. For damaged items, please include photos. See our [Refund Policy](/policies/refund-policy) and [Warranty Policy](/policies/terms-of-service#warranty) for full details.

## Technical Support

For technical questions about LZX modules — patching, signal flow, compatibility, or troubleshooting — email us at **support@lzxindustries.net**. Please include your module name, serial number (if applicable), and a description of the issue.
